— 01 Campaign Overview

Vision &
Direction

A campaign at the intersection of wealth, freedom, and the new African luxury narrative. QuickTrade does not merely sell a product — it sells a world.

Four cinematic chapters. Two iconic talents. One undeniable truth: financial freedom is the ultimate luxury. This treatment outlines the creative vision, production requirements, and narrative architecture for the QuickTrade aspirational campaign — a campaign designed to be felt before it is understood. Each video is a self-contained world, yet all four form a single, powerful statement about who QuickTrade's audience is — and who they are becoming.

Video 1 — The Rock Restaurant
Chapter I · Video 001

The
Rock

Location: The Rock Restaurant · Luxury Dining Experience
Mood & Theme

Luxury dining. Power moves. Soft wealth. Networking. Beautiful models in exquisite fashion. Expensive drinks flowing. The room is a stage — and everyone on it knows it. The feeling of a deal closed over Dom Pérignon, a smile that says more than words. Forex success made edible.

Cinematic Direction

Slow, deliberate movements. Golden hour light filtering through the windows. The camera drifts through the space like a guest at the table. Close-ups of hands holding glasses, watches catching light, eyes that hold the world's weight lightly. This is a world of earned ease.

Wardrobe & Styling

BigDaddyFatStax: bespoke navy or charcoal suit, luxury watch, pocket square. Cashmere: sharp tailored suit, statement watch, refined accessories — understated power. Models: high-fashion evening looks in neutral premium tones — champagne, ivory, slate.

Camera Direction

Sony FX9 / ARRI Mini LF. Slow motion at 120fps for drink pours and detail shots. Handheld for intimacy. Steadicam for sweeping room reveals. Macro lens for watch faces, currency, screen interfaces. No shaky cam — every movement intentional.

Lighting Direction

Warm amber practical lighting supplemented with soft HMI fill. Gold reflectors. Rim lighting on talent for separation. Candle elements practical and amplified. Shadows deep and intentional — wealth lives in contrast.

Music Vibe

Sophisticated neo-soul underscoring to deep house transitions. Strings. The kind of music you feel in your chest when you know you've made it. Think: Sade meets Kaytranada. Low BPM, high emotion. Subtle bass presence.

Video 2 — Sandton Hotel Penthouse
Chapter II · Video 002

Penthouse
Suite

Location: Luxury Hotel Sandton · Penthouse Level · Johannesburg
Mood & Theme

Morning routines of the wealthy. The penthouse as a world unto itself. Designer fashion. Champagne aesthetics at breakfast. The executive lifestyle — unhurried, deliberate, entirely intentional. Luxury travel energy: the sense that you could be anywhere in the world, and you chose here.

Cinematic Direction

Golden hour morning light. Floor-to-ceiling windows with Johannesburg as canvas. The camera lingers. Dutch angles on lifestyle elements. Overhead shots of the breakfast spread. Drone pull-back from window revealing the skyline. Intimate morning energy — this is private wealth, not public performance.

Wardrobe & Styling

BigDaddyFatStax: bespoke robe over designer loungewear, luxury slippers, statement watch. Cashmere: designer robe over tailored morning look, transitioning to sharp power outfit. Models: high-fashion in lifestyle contexts — architectural fashion that photographs like sculpture.

Camera Direction

ARRI Alexa Mini LF. Anamorphic lenses for cinematic width. Drone for establishing and pullbacks. Handheld for intimate moments. Slider for product reveal shots. 4K minimum, color corrected to warm champagne tones in post.

Lighting Direction

Natural morning light as hero source. Diffused through sheer curtains for a soft, dreamlike quality. Subtle warm practicals — bedside lamps, candles. Bounce boards to fill shadows without removing depth. The room should feel like it's glowing from within.

Music Vibe

Afro-soul meets luxury electronic. Think: serene piano over sub bass, building slowly. The kind of music that plays when you open your curtains on a day where everything is possible. Confident. Spacious. Rich.

Video 3 — Dinner In The Sky
Chapter III · Video 003

Above
The City

Location: Dinner In The Sky · Sandton · Johannesburg Skyline
Mood & Theme

The most cinematic chapter. Elite experiences that exist above the ordinary world — literally. Skyline luxury. Exclusive access. Night lights below like scattered gold. A celebration of success so extraordinary it cannot be contained by ground level. Dramatic. Breathtaking. Unforgettable.

Cinematic Direction

Drone choreography around the suspended platform. Upward-facing shots with city lights as backdrop. Counter-rotation of camera as platform moves. Hero shots against the open sky — talent above the clouds of the ordinary. This is the most visually ambitious chapter. Embrace the drama.

Wardrobe & Styling

Black tie. Full editorial luxury. BigDaddyFatStax: statement tuxedo, cufflinks, statement watch. Cashmere: sharp black-tie look, architectural tailoring, bold accessories. Models: show-stopping fashion that reads against the night sky. Every garment chosen for visual impact at distance and intimacy.

Camera Direction

Drone primary. DJI Inspire 3 for aerial choreography. A-cam gimbal-mounted on platform for stabilized close-ups. Wide anamorphic for the sky-and-city compositions. Night mode capability essential. Long lens compression on talent against city background. Every frame is a magazine cover.

Lighting Direction

Practical: table candles amplified, string lights on platform rigging. Supplemental: battery-powered LED panels, warm 3200K. Kino Flo for portrait fill. The city does the work — let it. The talent should feel like they belong to the sky. No overhead lighting — all comes from below and sides.

Music Vibe

Orchestral build. Strings and electronic elements in tension and release. Cinematic in the truest sense — this track should feel like a film score. Big, sweeping, emotional. The moment of arrival — this is the chapter where the audience feels the aspiration most viscerally.

Video 4 — Lanseria Private Jet
Chapter IV · Video 004

Departure
Protocol

Location: Lanseria International Airport · Private Terminal · Johannesburg
Mood & Theme

The final chapter. The most powerful. Global movement. Financial freedom made manifest in jet fuel and altitude. Departure energy — the energy of someone who has no limits on where they can go, because QuickTrade has no limits on what they can earn. Boss energy. Minimal. Powerful. Definitive.

Cinematic Direction

The tarmac as runway — fashion and aviation collide. Wide establishing shots of the aircraft. Talent walking toward the jet: purposeful, unhurried. The world behind them, everything ahead. Ending on the jet door closing, then cutting to the phone — QuickTrade active, altitude changing. Metaphor made literal.

Wardrobe & Styling

Clean. Architectural. Travel-luxury aesthetic. BigDaddyFatStax: tailored travel coat, designer luggage, understated power. Cashmere: minimal — structured blazer, luxury accessories, sunglasses. No excess. This is old money energy: everything says wealth without trying. The jet is the loudest thing in the frame.

Camera Direction

Ground-level for tarmac drama — wide angles that emphasize scale. Drone for the full aircraft reveal. Slow-motion at 240fps for coat movement, steps on tarmac, jet stairs. Interior shots: close, warm, intimate — the contrast of the vast exterior and the private interior. Lens flares from jet engines intentional.

Lighting Direction

Golden hour — the last light of day, or the breaking of a new dawn. Either reads as departure into something better. The jet as a silhouette against coloured sky. On-board: warm practical lighting, the intimacy of private aviation. Blue-hour secondary option for more cinematic drama.

Music Vibe

The crescendo. The entire campaign builds to this. A powerful, sweeping arrangement — electronic orchestration that sounds like freedom. Propulsive. Forward-moving. Bass that feels like jet engines. This is the track that plays when you close the chapter and begin the next one. Triumphant without being loud.

— 07 Cinematography Style

The
Visual Language

Camera References

ARRI Alexa Mini LF / Sony FX9. Both systems offer the full-frame sensor width required for the cinematic scope of this campaign. Backup: Sony Venice 2 for specific night sequences.

Lens Choices

Cooke S7 Anamorphic series as primary. Zeiss Supreme Primes for close-up work. 85mm portrait lenses for talent. The oval bokeh and characteristic flares of anamorphic lenses reinforce the cinematic premium positioning.

Colour Grading

Warm champagne tones as the campaign palette. Deep blacks with gold highlights. Skin tones elevated — rich, luminous, intentional. Reference: Beyoncé's "Black Is King." The grade should feel like fine whiskey — amber and deep.

Film Inspiration

Rolex Oyster Perpetual campaign films. Dior Sauvage short films. Ferrari Purosangue launch campaign. Jay-Z "4:44" visual album. Emmanuel Lubezki — one long, lingering take philosophy applied to 60-second commercial format.

Movement Style

Deliberate. Every move earns its right to exist. Slow pans. Controlled Steadicam. Drone choreography planned to the second. No handheld except for intentional intimacy moments. The camera moves like someone who owns the room.

Technical Notes

Shoot 4K minimum, 6K preferred. Frame rate: 24fps primary, 120fps for slow-motion inserts. Raw or Log C capture for maximum grade latitude. All footage to be colour-managed from capture through delivery. DCP and H.264 masters required.
